Esri Intern Hackathon - 3rd Place Winners!

Our rag-tag team of interns placed 3rd out of 17 participating intern teams.

NaviGator is a road-trip planning app that leverages a suite of Esri Developer Kits to help users plan out stops (hotels, food, entertainment, etc.) and drive times along their lengthy journeys.

We integrated the Yelp API to bring more information, details, and credibility to the points of interested we queried using Esri's ArcGIS Geocoding Python API.

The app was built using Django as the framework (which was new to all of us).
